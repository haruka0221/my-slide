Primary Text Strategy

Positioning Primary Text Analysis

Frequency
Confirm the occurrence of specific vocabulary and conceptual terms. A basic check on critical impressions.
Distribution
Examine skews across corpora, periods, and genres. Use topic modeling etc. to identify tendencies.
Comparison
Compare differences across genres, authors, and periods to relativize the premises of a reading.
Authorship attribution
Verify co-authorship, attribution, and stylistic differences to check the bibliographic facts that criticism takes for granted.
